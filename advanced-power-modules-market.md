<p><p>The Advanced Power Modules market includes Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) and Power Integrated Modules (PIMs). IPMs integrate power devices, gate drivers, and protection features into a compact package, enhancing efficiency and reducing design complexity in applications like motor control and renewable energy systems. PIMs combine multiple power components, enabling seamless performance and thermal management in high-power applications. Both types play a crucial role in modern electronics, promoting miniaturization and efficiency while addressing the growing demand for sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
