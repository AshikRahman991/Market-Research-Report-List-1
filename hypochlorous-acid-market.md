<p><p>The hypochlorous acid market primarily consists of two types: sodium hypochlorite and calcium hypochlorite. Sodium hypochlorite is a widely used disinfectant and bleaching agent, often found in household cleaning products and wastewater treatment. Calcium hypochlorite, on the other hand, is a solid disinfectant commonly used for water chlorination in swimming pools and municipal water systems.
