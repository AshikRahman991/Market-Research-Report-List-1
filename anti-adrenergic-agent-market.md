<p><strong>The Anti Adrenergic Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>α Blockers</li><li>β Blockers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-adrenergic agent market is primarily categorized into alpha (α) blockers and beta (β) blockers, both of which are crucial in treating cardiovascular conditions. Alpha blockers focus on blocking adrenergic receptors in blood vessels, leading to vasodilation and reduced blood pressure. In contrast, beta blockers inhibit the effects of adrenaline on heart receptors, helping to lower heart rate and manage arrhythmias. These agents play significant roles in treating hypertension, heart failure, and anxiety disorders, contributing to the overall growth of the pharmaceutical market.</p></p>
