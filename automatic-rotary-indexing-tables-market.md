<p><p>Automatic rotary indexing tables are classified into three main types based on their drive mechanisms. Servo-driven gear head tables offer high precision and flexibility for various applications, utilizing servo motors for smooth operation. Servo-driven barrel-cam tables provide rapid indexing and versatility, ideal for complex tasks with varying cycle times. Cam-driven indexing tables, on the other hand, use mechanical cams for reliable and cost-effective solutions, best suited for repetitive tasks with lower precision requirements. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
