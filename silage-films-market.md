<p><p>Silage films are crucial for preserving forage and maintaining nutritional quality. The market is primarily divided into three types: LLDPE, LDPE, and Others. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) offers enhanced strength and stretchability, making it ideal for tough outdoor conditions. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is more flexible and has lower tensile strength, suitable for lighter applications. The "Others" category includes alternative materials like biodegradable films or specialty polymers, catering to specific agricultural needs and sustainability goals in silage production.</p></p>
