<p><strong>The Smartphone Screen Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tempered Glass</li><li>Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET)</li><li>Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone screen protector market offers three main types: Tempered Glass, Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), and Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU). Tempered Glass provides superior durability, scratch resistance, and clarity, mimicking the feel of the original screen. PET protectors are thin, lightweight, and affordable but less durable. TPU protectors are flexible, self-healing, and offer better impact resistance, making them ideal for curved screens. Each type caters to different user needs, balancing protection, cost, and usability for smartphones.</p></p>
