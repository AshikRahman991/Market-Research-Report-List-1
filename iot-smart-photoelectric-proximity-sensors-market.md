<p><p>The IoT Smart Photoelectric Proximity Sensors market encompasses various types of sensors that utilize light to detect the presence of objects. Through-beam sensors consist of a transmitter and receiver positioned opposite each other, providing high detection accuracy over long distances. Retro-reflective sensors use a reflector to bounce the emitted light back to the sensor, enabling detection without a direct line of sight. Diffuse sensors emit light and detect it reflected off nearby objects, offering versatility in various applications.</p></p>
