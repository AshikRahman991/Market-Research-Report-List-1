<p><ul><li>Immersion Types</li><li>Water Bath Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial sous vide machines market primarily consists of immersion and water bath types. Immersion sous vide machines use a heating element and circulator to maintain a precise water temperature, ensuring even cooking of food in vacuum-sealed bags. Water bath types, on the other hand, are larger containers that hold the water bath, allowing for bulk cooking and accommodating multiple bags at once. Both types cater to restaurants and catering services, enhancing food preparation efficiency and consistency.</p></p>
