<p><p>Clad metals involve combining different metal layers for enhanced properties. The Ni/Cu type features nickel-clad copper for corrosion resistance. Ni/Cu/Ni adds an outer nickel layer for additional protection. Cu/Al combines copper and aluminum, offering lightweight and conductivity benefits. Fe/Cu integrates copper with iron, improving strength and conductivity. Precious metal clads utilize gold or silver for high-value applications, while the "Other" category includes unique combinations tailored for specific industry needs, expanding the versatility of clad metal solutions.</p></p>
