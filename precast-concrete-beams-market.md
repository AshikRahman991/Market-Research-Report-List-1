<p><ul><li>Frame Beam</li><li>Non-frame Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precast concrete beams market includes two main types: frame beams and non-frame beams. Frame beams are integral to structural frameworks, providing essential support and load distribution in buildings and infrastructure. They are commonly used in multi-story constructions and bridges. Non-frame beams, on the other hand, serve secondary or non-structural purposes, such as decorative elements, partition walls, or lightweight applications. Both types are prefabricated off-site, ensuring quality, efficiency, and faster construction timelines compared to traditional methods.</p></p>
