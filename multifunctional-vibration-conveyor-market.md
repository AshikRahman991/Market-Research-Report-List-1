<p><ul><li>Natural Frequency Types</li><li>Forced Vibration Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunctional vibration conveyor market is primarily categorized into two types: natural frequency and forced vibration types. Natural frequency conveyors operate at their inherent frequency, allowing for energy-efficient material handling without external forces. In contrast, forced vibration conveyors utilize external motors to generate consistent vibrations, providing better control over material flow and discharge rates. Each type serves specific applications, with natural frequency conveyors favored for simplicity and energy savings, while forced vibration models are preferred for precise material movement.</p></p>
