<p><p>Opto MOSFET relays are semiconductor devices used for switching applications, categorized into three main market types: AC, DC, and AC/DC. AC relays handle alternating current, making them suitable for applications requiring isolation and control of voltage fluctuations. DC relays, on the other hand, are designed for direct current applications, providing efficient switching with low on-resistance. AC/DC relays combine the capabilities of both, allowing them to operate with either type of current, offering versatility for diverse electronic circuit needs.</p></p>
