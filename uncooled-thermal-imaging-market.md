<p><p>The uncooled thermal imaging market is categorized into four primary types based on infrared wavelength: Short Wave IR (SWIR), Mid Wave IR (MWIR), Long Wave IR (LWIR), and Far Wave IR (FWIR). SWIR technology focuses on detecting shorter infrared wavelengths, often used in industrial applications. MWIR offers enhanced sensitivity for military and aerospace. LWIR is widely used for building inspections and surveillance due to its effectiveness in detecting heat. FWIR, though less common, is used in research and advanced scientific applications.</p></p>
