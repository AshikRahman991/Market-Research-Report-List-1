Cationic agents, with a positive charge, are effective in promoting stability in pigments and dyes. "Others" encompasses non-ionic agents, which are neutral and suitable for diverse applications, including pharmaceuticals and food products. Each type plays a crucial role in improving product performance across various industries.</p></p>
