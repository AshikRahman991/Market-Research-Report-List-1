<p><strong>The Low-Pressure Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-pressure casting machine market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical low-pressure casting machines operate by using gravitational force to fill molds from the bottom, ensuring efficient material distribution and reducing defects. Conversely, horizontal low-pressure casting machines utilize a side-entry casting method, allowing for a more compact design and easier access for maintenance. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, optimizing production efficiency and improving casting quality across various industries.</p></p>
