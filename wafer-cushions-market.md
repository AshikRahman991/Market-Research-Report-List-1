<p><p>The wafer cushions market includes conductive polyethylene and anti-static polyurethane cushions, each designed for specific applications in semiconductor handling. Conductive polyethylene cushions prevent electrostatic discharge, ensuring safe transportation of sensitive components. They are commonly used in environments where static-sensitive devices are present. Anti-static polyurethane cushions provide additional protection against mechanical damage while also dissipating static charges. These cushions are ideal for delicate electronic parts, enhancing both safety and performance during shipping and storage in cleanroom settings.</p></p>
