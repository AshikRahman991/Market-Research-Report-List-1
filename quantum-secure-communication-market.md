<p><ul><li>Quantum Key Distribution</li><li>Quantum Teleportation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantum secure communication market is primarily divided into two types: Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and Quantum Teleportation. QKD utilizes quantum mechanics to securely distribute encryption keys between parties, ensuring that any eavesdropping attempts are detectable. In contrast, Quantum Teleportation allows for the instantaneous transmission of quantum states between distant locations, facilitating secure information transfer without physically moving the particles involved. Both methods leverage quantum principles to enhance security in communications, addressing vulnerabilities in traditional systems.</p></p>
