<p><strong>The SLS Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting(SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SLS additive manufacturing market includes Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM) technologies. SLM utilizes high-powered lasers to fuse fine metal powders layer by layer, ideal for producing dense, complex parts, primarily in industries like aerospace and medical. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powders, catering to similar sectors but offering benefits like faster build rates and the ability to work with reactive materials. Both methods enhance design flexibility and efficiency in production.</p></p>
