<p><ul><li>Solid NBR Rubber</li><li>NBR Latex</li><li>Hydrogenated NBR (HNBR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrile elastomers market comprises three main types: solid NBR rubber, NBR latex, and hydrogenated NBR (HNBR). Solid NBR rubber is widely used for its oil-resistant properties in automotive and industrial applications. NBR latex, a water-based form, is utilized in coatings and adhesive products for its flexibility and durability. HNBR, which offers enhanced heat, ozone, and chemical resistance, is preferred for demanding applications like automotive seals and gaskets. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
