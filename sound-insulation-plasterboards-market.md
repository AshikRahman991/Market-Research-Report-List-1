<p><strong>The Sound Insulation Plasterboards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tapered-Edged</li><li>Square-Edged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sound insulation plasterboards market is categorized into two main types: tapered-edged and square-edged boards. Tapered-edged plasterboards feature beveled edges that allow for seamless jointing, leading to smoother finishes and enhanced sound insulation when layered. In contrast, square-edged plasterboards have flat edges, making them suitable for basic applications and simpler installations. Both types are designed to reduce sound transmission, with tapered-edged boards often preferred for high-performance acoustic environments due to their better jointing capabilities and overall effectiveness.</p></p>
