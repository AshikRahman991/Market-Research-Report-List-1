<p><p>The Air Gripper market features three main types: Friction Grip, Cradled Grip, and Encapsulated Grip. Friction Grip relies on surface contact to hold objects securely, making it ideal for flat items. Cradled Grip uses a contoured design to support irregular shapes, providing stability during handling. Encapsulated Grip employs a protective enclosure around the object, safeguarding delicate items while maintaining a secure hold. Each type is designed to cater to specific applications, enhancing efficiency in automated processes.</p></p>
