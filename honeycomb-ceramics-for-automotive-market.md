<p><strong>The Honeycomb Ceramics for Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TWC</li><li>GFP</li><li>DOC</li><li>ASC</li><li>DFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Honeycomb ceramics are advanced materials used in automotive exhaust systems to reduce emissions and improve efficiency. Types include:</p><p>1. **TWC (Three-Way Catalyst)**: Converts harmful gases (CO, NOx, HC) into less harmful substances (CO2, N2, H2O) in gasoline engines.</p><p>2. **GFP (Gasoline Particulate Filter)**: Captures soot particles from gasoline direct injection engines.</p><p>3. **DOC (Diesel Oxidation Catalyst)**: Oxidizes CO and HC into CO2 and H2O in diesel engines.</p><p>4. **ASC (Ammonia Slip Catalyst)**: Reduces excess ammonia from SCR systems in diesel vehicles.</p><p>5. **DFP (Diesel Particulate Filter)**: Traps and burns soot from diesel exhaust, ensuring cleaner emissions.</p></p>
