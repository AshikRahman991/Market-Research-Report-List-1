<p><p>Dental digital radiography systems encompass various types, primarily including ordinary radiography systems and panoramic radiography systems. Ordinary radiography systems provide detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures, facilitating precise diagnosis and treatment planning. In contrast, panoramic radiography systems offer a comprehensive, wide-angle view of the entire dental arch, capturing both upper and lower jaws in a single image. This enables dentists to assess overall dental health and detect issues that may not be visible with traditional imaging methods.</p></p>
