<p><strong>The Fire Resistant Hydraulic Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant hydraulic fluids market is categorized into four types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing, offering excellent fire protection but low lubricity. HFB fluids combine water and organic compounds, providing moderate fire resistance. HFC fluids are water-containing synthetic fluids that offer high lubricity and good fire resistance, while HFD fluids are non-water-containing synthetic fluids designed for severe fire hazard environments, offering superior lubricating properties and enhanced protection against ignition and combustion.</p></p>
