<p><p>Specialty Grade DWP (Dissolving Wood Pulp) markets are categorized into alkali wood pulp or sulfate wood pulp and sulfite wood pulp. Alkali wood pulp, produced through the kraft process, is favored for its high purity and strength, often used in producing high-quality textiles and chemicals. Conversely, sulfite wood pulp, created through the sulfite process, has a different cellulose structure that is suitable for specialized applications but typically has lower strength. Both types cater to niche markets requiring specific qualities for end products.</p></p>
