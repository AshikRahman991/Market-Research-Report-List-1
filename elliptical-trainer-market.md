<p><strong>The Elliptical Trainer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Drive</li><li>Rear Drive</li><li>Center Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elliptical trainer market is categorized into three main types: front drive, rear drive, and center drive. Front drive ellipticals feature the wheel at the front, providing a more natural motion but can take up more space. Rear drive models have the wheel at the back, offering a smoother glide and better stability. Center drive ellipticals place the wheel at the center, allowing for a compact design and balanced motion, making them suitable for smaller spaces while still providing an effective workout.</p></p>
