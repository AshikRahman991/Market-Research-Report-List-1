<p><p>The vulcanizing agent market comprises two main types: hardeners and accelerators. Hardeners, such as sulfur or peroxide, facilitate cross-linking in rubber, enhancing durability and thermal stability. Accelerators, on the other hand, speed up the vulcanization process, improving efficiency and reducing production times. Both additives play crucial roles in determining the physical properties of rubber products, catering to various industries, including automotive and construction, by ensuring superior performance and longevity of rubber materials.</p></p>
