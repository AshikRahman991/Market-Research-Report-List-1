<p><p>Cord-end ferrules are specialized components used in electrical and electronic applications to securely terminate wires. The market can be divided into two main types: insulated and non-insulated ferrules. Insulated ferrules are coated with a protective layer that enhances safety and prevents accidental contact with conductive parts, making them suitable for high-voltage environments. In contrast, non-insulated ferrules provide a more compact solution without the insulation layer, ideal for applications where space is limited and electrical exposure is controlled.</p></p>
