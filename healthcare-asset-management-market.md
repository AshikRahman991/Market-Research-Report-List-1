<p><p>Healthcare asset management encompasses various technologies to enhance tracking and utilization of medical equipment. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) uses radio waves for real-time tracking, ensuring efficient asset management. RTLS (Real-Time Location Systems) provides precise location data through various technologies, improving operational efficiency. Ultrasound technology leverages sound waves for locating assets, offering a reliable alternative. Infrared systems use light waves to track equipment, providing accurate positioning in enclosed spaces. Each technology enhances inventory management and reduces operational costs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
