<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 diagnostic tests market includes various types such as Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results, allowing for immediate diagnosis using a small sample, making them ideal for point-of-care settings. ELISA tests detect antibodies or antigens with higher sensitivity and specificity, suitable for large-scale screening. Neutralization Assays measure the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, crucial for vaccine efficacy assessment and understanding immune responses.</p></p>
