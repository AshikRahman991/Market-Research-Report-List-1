<p><strong>The Illumination Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Back-Illuminated Sensor</li><li>Front-Illuminated Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The illumination sensor market comprises two primary types: back-illuminated sensors and front-illuminated sensors. Back-illuminated sensors feature a design where light hits the sensor layer from behind, enhancing sensitivity and low-light performance, making them ideal for applications like smartphone cameras and advanced imaging systems. In contrast, front-illuminated sensors capture light directly on the surface, offering simpler construction and often lower costs, suitable for basic cameras and consumer electronics. Each type caters to specific requirements in imaging technology.</p></p>
