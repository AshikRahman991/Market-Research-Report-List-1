<p><p>The mini Bluetooth speakers market can be categorized into three types based on power supply: AC/DC Bluetooth speakers, AC Bluetooth speakers, and DC Bluetooth speakers. AC/DC Bluetooth speakers can operate on both alternating current and direct current, offering versatility for home and outdoor use. AC Bluetooth speakers require a constant wall power source, making them ideal for stationary setups. In contrast, DC Bluetooth speakers rely on battery power, emphasizing portability and convenience for outdoor activities. These variations cater to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
