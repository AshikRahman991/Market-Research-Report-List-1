<p><p>The biomass briquette fuel market comprises primarily two types: bulk biomass briquettes and biomass pellets. Bulk biomass briquettes are larger, often cylindrical or square in shape, and are designed for efficient combustion in industrial and commercial applications. They provide a cost-effective solution for heating and energy generation. Biomass pellets, on the other hand, are smaller, more uniform particles that offer high energy density and are suitable for residential heating systems, providing a cleaner and more efficient fuel option.</p></p>
