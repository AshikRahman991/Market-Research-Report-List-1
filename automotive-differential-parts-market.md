<p><p>The automotive differential market includes various components essential for vehicle performance. **Pinion Bearings** support the pinion gear, ensuring smooth rotation. **Spider & Side Gears** facilitate power distribution between wheels during turns. **Side Bearings** stabilize the differential case, reducing friction. The **Differential Case** houses internal components, maintaining structural integrity. **Differential Drive Gear** transfers torque from the driveshaft to the wheels. **Other** components include seals, shims, and bolts, ensuring durability and efficiency. Each part plays a critical role in optimizing traction, handling, and overall drivetrain functionality.</p></p>
