<p><ul><li>Flate Face (FF)</li><li>Raised Face (RF)</li><li>Ring Type Joint (RTJ)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Slip-On Flanges market encompasses several types, including Flat Face (FF), Raised Face (RF), and Ring Type Joint (RTJ) flanges. Flat Face flanges have a flat surface that allows for even distribution of pressure, ideal for non-metallic applications. Raised Face flanges feature a raised area for better sealing under pressure, commonly used in high-pressure systems. Ring Type Joint flanges are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature environments, utilizing a metal ring for a reliable seal, making them suitable for critical applications.</p></p>
