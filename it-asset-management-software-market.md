<p><p>IT Asset Management (ITAM) software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based ITAM solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making them suitable for organizations seeking quick deployment and remote accessibility. On-premise ITAM software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company’s infrastructure, providing greater control over data and customization options, but often requires higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
