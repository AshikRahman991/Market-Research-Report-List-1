<p><strong>The Radiation Detection Materials and Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-Filled Detectors</li><li>Scintillators Detectors</li><li>Solid-State Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation detection materials and equipment market comprises three main types: gas-filled detectors, scintillator detectors, and solid-state detectors. Gas-filled detectors use ionization of gas to measure radiation, offering high sensitivity. Scintillator detectors utilize materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, making them effective for various applications. Solid-state detectors rely on semiconductor materials to convert radiation into measurable electrical signals, providing precision and compact designs. Each type serves different needs in safety, security, and scientific research.</p></p>
