<p><p>IT Asset Management (ITAM) software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based ITAM software is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy accessibility and scalability while minimizing infrastructure costs. It enables real-time updates and collaboration across teams. In contrast, on-premise ITAM software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering more control over data security and customization but often requiring higher initial investment and maintenance efforts. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
