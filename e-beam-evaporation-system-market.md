<p><p>The E-beam evaporation system market is divided into three main types: Single Pocket, Rotary Pocket, and Linear Pocket systems. Single Pocket systems are designed for small-scale applications, using one evaporation source for uniform deposition. Rotary Pocket systems feature multiple pockets, allowing for simultaneous deposition of different materials, enhancing efficiency. Linear Pocket systems utilize a linear arrangement of evaporation sources, enabling longer substrates to be coated in a continuous process, making them ideal for high-throughput manufacturing in various industries.</p></p>
