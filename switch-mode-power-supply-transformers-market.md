<p><p>The Switch Mode Power Supply (SMPS) transformers market is categorized into two main types: single-excited and double-excited transformers. Single-excited transformers utilize one winding for input and another for output, making them simpler and more cost-effective for low-power applications. In contrast, double-excited transformers have primary and secondary windings independently supplied with energy, allowing for improved efficiency and higher power handling capabilities. Each type serves specific applications, influencing their demand and market dynamics based on performance and operational requirements.</p></p>
