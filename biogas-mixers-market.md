<p><strong>The Biogas Mixers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Mixers</li><li>Dry-installed Mixers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biogas mixers market is primarily divided into two types: submersible mixers and dry-installed mixers. Submersible mixers operate underwater, effectively blending materials in anaerobic digesters to enhance biogas production. They are typically energy-efficient and space-saving. On the other hand, dry-installed mixers are positioned above the substrate and connect to the tank via pipes. These mixers are favored for their easy maintenance and ability to handle larger volumes. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing anaerobic digestion processes.</p></p>
