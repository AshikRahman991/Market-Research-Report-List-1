<p><ul><li>Cryogenic Distillation</li><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food grade liquid nitrogen market includes two primary production methods: cryogenic distillation and pressure swing adsorption. Cryogenic distillation involves cooling and separating gases at extremely low temperatures, ensuring high purity levels for food applications. In contrast, pressure swing adsorption utilizes pressure changes to separate nitrogen from air, offering a more energy-efficient and cost-effective solution. Both methods cater to the growing demand for liquid nitrogen in food preservation, packaging, and freezing processes, highlighting their significance in the food industry.</p></p>
