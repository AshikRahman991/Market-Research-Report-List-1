<p><p>The termite drug market encompasses various types used for pest control, each with distinct characteristics. Bifenthrins are synthetic pyrethroids known for their effectiveness in killing termites on contact. Borates, derived from naturally occurring minerals, offer a long-lasting solution by affecting termite digestion. Sulfuryl fluoride is a highly effective gas fumigant that penetrates structures to eliminate infestations. Other products may include biological control agents and insect growth regulators, providing diverse options for consumers in termite management.</p></p>
