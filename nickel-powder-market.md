<p><strong>The Nickel Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbonyl Nickel Powder</li><li>Electrolytic Nickel Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel powder market primarily consists of two types: carbonyl nickel powder and electrolytic nickel powder. Carbonyl nickel powder is produced through the carbonyl process, resulting in high-purity, fine particles ideal for applications in electronics and catalysts. In contrast, electrolytic nickel powder is generated through electrolysis and is typically coarser, making it suitable for battery production and metal coatings. Both types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and chemical sectors, driving demand for advanced nickel-based materials.</p></p>
