<p><strong>The Knitted Cloth Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Warp Knitting</li><li>Weft Knitting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knitted cloth market is primarily divided into two types: warp knitting and weft knitting. Warp knitting involves the creation of fabric by interlocking yarns vertically, resulting in strong, stable materials often used for lace, undergarments, and technical textiles. In contrast, weft knitting interlaces yarns horizontally, allowing for more elasticity and versatility, commonly found in garments like sweaters and t-shirts. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs and play significant roles in the fashion and textile industries.</p></p>
