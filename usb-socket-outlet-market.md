<p><p>The USB socket outlet market can be categorized into two main types: in-wall type and extension cord type. In-wall type USB socket outlets are permanently installed into walls, providing a seamless and aesthetic charging solution for homes and offices. They typically feature multiple USB ports and complement traditional power outlets. In contrast, extension cord type USB outlets are portable and feature USB ports integrated into power strips or cords, allowing users to charge multiple devices in varied locations while offering flexibility and convenience.</p></p>
