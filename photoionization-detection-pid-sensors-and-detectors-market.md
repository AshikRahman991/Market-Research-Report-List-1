<p><p>Photoionization Detection (PID) Sensors and Detectors are advanced devices used to detect and measure volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other hazardous gases. These sensors operate by exposing a sample to ultraviolet (UV) light, ionizing the molecules present. The resulting charged particles are then measured, providing qualitative and quantitative data on gas concentration. PID sensors are prized for their fast response times, sensitivity to low concentration levels, and ability to detect a wide range of organic compounds.</p><p>The PID Sensors and Detectors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period.
