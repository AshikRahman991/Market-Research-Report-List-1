<p><p>The semipermeable membrane market comprises various types, including composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes are engineered by combining two or more materials to enhance performance and selectivity, making them suitable for diverse applications like water purification and gas separation. Asymmetric membranes feature a porous structure with a thin selective layer, providing high permeability and fouling resistance. Both types are vital in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and environmental management, driving innovation in separation technologies.</p></p>
