<p><p>The 5G spectrum market includes several key types, primarily C-Band and Millimeter Wave (mmWave) bands. C-Band, typically ranging from 3.3 to 4.2 GHz, balances coverage and capacity, making it suitable for widespread urban deployment. In contrast, the mmWave band, usually from 24 GHz to 100 GHz, offers exceptionally high speeds and capacity but has limited range and penetration. These two spectrum types cater to different 5G applications, optimizing performance based on user density and geographical challenges.</p></p>
