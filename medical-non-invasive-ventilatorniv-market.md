<p><p>The Medical Non-Invasive Ventilator (NIV) market primarily includes two types: CPAP and BiPAP ventilators. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) devices deliver a constant airflow to keep airways open, primarily used for sleep apnea. In contrast, BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) machines provide two distinct pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, facilitating more complex respiratory needs. Both types are crucial in managing respiratory issues without the need for invasive procedures, enhancing patient comfort and outcomes.</p></p>
