<p><p>Cloud GIS markets encompass three primary service models: SaaS, PaaS, and IaaS. SaaS provides GIS applications accessible via the cloud, allowing users to utilize mapping and analytics tools without extensive infrastructure. PaaS offers a platform for developers to build custom GIS applications, facilitating integration with other services and enabling scalability. IaaS supplies the underlying infrastructure, such as servers and storage, necessary for hosting GIS solutions, allowing organizations to manage their own applications and data while leveraging cloud resources.</p></p>
