<p><strong>The Surgical Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image intensifier C-arms</li><li>Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical Imaging Systems market is primarily divided into two types: Image Intensifier C-arms and Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms). Image Intensifier C-arms utilize a traditional imaging technology that enhances visibility in surgical settings by intensifying the X-ray image. In contrast, FPD C-arms use flat panel detectors, offering superior image quality, lower radiation doses, and greater flexibility in positioning. Both types play crucial roles in real-time imaging during minimally invasive procedures, enhancing surgical precision and outcomes.</p></p>
