<p><p>The Industrial Gas Alarm market encompasses various sensor types, primarily including catalytic and infrared optical sensors. Catalytic sensors detect combustible gases by measuring changes in resistance due to gas oxidation on a catalyst, making them suitable for gases like methane. In contrast, infrared optical sensors use light absorption characteristics of specific gases to detect them, providing high sensitivity and selectivity for hydrocarbons. Both types are vital for ensuring safety in industrial environments by providing reliable gas leak detection and monitoring.</p></p>
