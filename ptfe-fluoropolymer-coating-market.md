<p><p>PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) fluoropolymer coatings are categorized primarily into industrial grade and food grade. Industrial grade coatings are designed for high-performance applications, providing superior chemical resistance and non-stick properties for machinery, pipes, and equipment used in manufacturing processes. In contrast, food grade PTFE coatings meet stringent safety regulations, ensuring they are safe for direct contact with food. This type is commonly used in cookware and food processing equipment, providing a non-reactive surface that enhances hygiene and ease of cleaning.</p></p>
