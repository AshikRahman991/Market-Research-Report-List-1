<p><p>The HV Switchgear market comprises various types based on insulation medium. Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS) utilizes air as the main insulating medium, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for outdoor installations. Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) employs sulfur hexafluoride (SF6) gas for insulation and excellent performance in compact systems, ideal for urban environments. Oil Insulated Switchgear uses oil as both an insulator and coolant, providing high reliability. Other types include hybrid systems and encapsulated technologies, catering to specific applications and environmental conditions.</p></p>
