<p><p>Physiotherapy examination tables are essential for patient assessment and treatment, available in electric, manual, and hydraulic types. Electric tables offer adjustable height and positioning via motorized controls, ensuring precision and ease of use. Manual tables rely on hand-crank mechanisms for adjustments, providing durability and cost-effectiveness. Hydraulic tables use fluid pressure for smooth height adjustments, balancing functionality and affordability. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, with electric tables emphasizing convenience, manual tables focusing on simplicity, and hydraulic tables offering a middle ground.</p></p>
