<p><strong>The qPCR Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-based Reagents</li><li>Probe & Primer-based Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The qPCR reagents market is primarily divided into two types: dye-based reagents and probe & primer-based reagents. Dye-based reagents, such as SYBR Green, are simple and cost-effective, providing real-time quantification of DNA amplification by emitting fluorescence during PCR cycles. In contrast, probe & primer-based reagents utilize specific probes, like TaqMan or molecular beacons, enhancing specificity and sensitivity by binding to target sequences. Both types cater to diverse applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and genetic analysis.</p></p>
