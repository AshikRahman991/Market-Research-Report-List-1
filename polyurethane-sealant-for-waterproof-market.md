<p><p>Polyurethane sealants for waterproofing are available in two primary types: single-component and multi-component. Single-component sealants are ready to use straight from the container, curing through moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. Multi-component sealants require mixing before use, allowing for greater customization and enhanced performance properties, ideal for demanding environments. Both types offer excellent adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to water, making them suitable for various applications in construction and industrial sectors.</p></p>
