<p><p>The metal-ceramic dental crown and bridge market is divided into two main types: dental crowns and dental bridges. Dental crowns are used to restore damaged teeth by covering them entirely, providing strength and aesthetic appeal. They are often employed after root canals or to support large fillings. Dental bridges, on the other hand, are designed to fill gaps left by missing teeth, anchoring to adjacent teeth for stability. Both forms combine durability with a natural appearance, making them popular in restorative dentistry.</p></p>
