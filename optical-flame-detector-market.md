<p><p>The optical flame detector market primarily comprises two types: infrared (IR) detectors and ultraviolet (UV) detectors. IR detectors sense heat emitted by flames, effectively identifying hydrocarbon-based fires, while UV detectors respond to the ultraviolet radiation released by flames, offering rapid detection of various fire types, including those from combustible gases. Both types are widely used in industrial applications for their capability to minimize false alarms, provide quick response times, and enhance safety in hazardous environments by reliably detecting flames.</p></p>
