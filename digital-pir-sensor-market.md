<p><p>The Digital PIR (Passive Infrared) Sensor market is categorized into three main types: Single Element, Dual Element, and Others. Single Element sensors detect infrared radiation from a single point, making them cost-effective but less sensitive to movement direction. Dual Element sensors enhance detection by utilizing two sensing elements, allowing for better motion tracking and improved accuracy. The "Others" category includes advanced variations like smart sensors with integrated technology for enhanced functionality, catering to diverse applications in security and automation systems.</p></p>
