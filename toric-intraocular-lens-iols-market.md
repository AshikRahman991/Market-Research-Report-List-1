<p><p>Toric Intraocular Lenses (IOLs) are specialized lens implants used during cataract surgery to correct astigmatism, a condition that causes blurred vision due to an irregular curvature of the cornea. Unlike standard IOLs, toric IOLs have varying optical power and curvature, allowing for improved vision correction post-surgery. The increasing prevalence of cataracts and the growing aging population are key factors driving the demand for toric IOLs. </p><p>The Toric Intraocular Lens IOLs Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.80% during the forecast period.
