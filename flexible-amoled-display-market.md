<p><p>The Flexible AMOLED display market is dominated by two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) displays and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) displays. AMOLED displays utilize an active switching technology, allowing for higher resolution, faster refresh rates, and better image quality, making them ideal for smartphones and high-end devices. Conversely, PMOLED displays use passive control, offering simplicity and low cost but limited resolution and size scalability. Both types cater to various applications, from wearable tech to innovative display designs.</p></p>
