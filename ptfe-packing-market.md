Thin PTFE packing is typically used for applications requiring precision sealing and flexibility, such as in pumps and valves, ensuring minimal leakage. In contrast, thick PTFE packing offers enhanced durability and resistance to wear, making it suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature environments. Both types serve essential roles in various industries, including chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, to maintain system integrity and efficiency.</p></p>
