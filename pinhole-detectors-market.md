<p><p>The pinhole detectors market includes high voltage holiday detectors and low voltage pinhole detectors, each serving different applications in non-destructive testing. High voltage holiday detectors are typically used for detecting defects in thick protective coatings on pipelines and tanks, operating at higher voltage levels to identify pinholes and irregularities. Conversely, low voltage pinhole detectors are utilized for thinner coatings and more sensitive applications, providing precision in identifying surface defects without damaging the material under examination.</p></p>
