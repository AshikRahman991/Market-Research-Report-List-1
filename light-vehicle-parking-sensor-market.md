<p><p>The light vehicle parking sensor market is categorized into front view and rear view systems. In contrast, rear view parking sensors are designed to aid in reversing, alerting drivers to potential hazards behind the vehicle. Both types utilize ultrasonic or electromagnetic technologies to provide real-time feedback, significantly improving parking accuracy and reducing accident risks.</p></p>
