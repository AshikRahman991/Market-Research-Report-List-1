<p><p>The residential patio heaters market comprises three main types: propane, electric, and natural gas. Propane heaters are portable and ideal for outdoor spaces without electrical access, providing effective heating through gas fuel. Electric heaters, suitable for smaller areas, offer convenience and ease of installation, often featuring adjustable settings. Natural gas heaters are typically more permanent fixtures, requiring a gas line, and are favored for their efficiency in heating larger outdoor spaces consistently. Each type meets varying consumer preferences in functionality and convenience.</p></p>
