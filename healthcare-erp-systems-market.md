<p><p>Healthcare ERP systems can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ERP systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and ease of updates. Conversely, on-premises ERP systems are installed locally on healthcare organizations' servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant initial investment and maintenance. Both types aim to streamline operations, improve data management, and enhance patient care within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
