<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) stabilizers market encompasses several types, including Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), UV absorbers, quenchers, and other specialized products. HALS are effective in preventing polymer degradation by scavenging free radicals. UV absorbers absorb damaging UV radiation, converting it into harmless heat. Quenchers are additives that dissipate energy from excited molecules to reduce oxidative degradation. Other market types may include advanced formulations and hybrid stabilizers, catering to specific applications in plastics, coatings, and automotive industries, enhancing product longevity.</p></p>
