<p><strong>The Automotive CFRP Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive CFRP (carbon fiber reinforced polymer) market comprises two main types: thermosetting and thermoplastic CFRP. Thermosetting CFRP involves resins that cure permanently when heated, offering excellent dimensional stability and rigidity, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, thermoplastic CFRP uses resins that can be repeatedly softened and reshaped with heat, enabling easier manufacturing and recyclability. Both types provide lightweight solutions to enhance fuel efficiency and performance, catering to the evolving needs of the automotive industry.</p></p>
