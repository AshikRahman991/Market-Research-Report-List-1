<p><ul><li>Calcium Process</li><li>Sodium Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Calcium Hypochlorite market can be segmented into two main types based on the production processes: the Calcium Process and the Sodium Process. The Calcium Process involves reacting calcium hydroxide with chlorine gas, yielding a product that is typically used for water disinfection and bleaching. In contrast, the Sodium Process combines sodium hypochlorite with calcium hydroxide to produce calcium hypochlorite. Each method has distinct characteristics, influencing factors such as purity, cost, and application suitability in various industries.</p></p>
