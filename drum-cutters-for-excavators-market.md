<p><p>Drum cutters for excavators are specialized attachments used for precise material removal and rock excavation. The market is categorized into two main types: transverse and axial. Transverse drum cutters feature a horizontal drum with sharp, rotating teeth, ideal for cutting through hard materials like concrete and rock, while allowing for controlled excavation. Axial drum cutters, on the other hand, have a vertical orientation, enabling applications such as trenching and tunnel boring with increased maneuverability. Each type serves unique operational requirements.</p></p>
