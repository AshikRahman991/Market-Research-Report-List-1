<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roller motor market encompasses two main types: electric and pneumatic. Electric roller motors use electrical energy to drive rollers, providing high efficiency, precision, and ease of integration into automated systems, making them ideal for applications requiring continuous motion. In contrast, pneumatic roller motors utilize compressed air, offering high torque and rapid response times, suitable for environments where electricity may pose a risk. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and material handling, enabling optimized operational efficiency.</p></p>
