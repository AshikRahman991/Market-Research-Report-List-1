<p><p>Powder In-Mould Coating (PIMC) encompasses the use of powdered coatings applied directly in the mould during the manufacturing process. This technique is particularly relevant in the Sheet Moulding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Moulding Compound (BMC) markets. SMC involves pre-impregnated sheets used for large moldings, while BMC consists of highly viscous compounds ideal for intricate shapes. Both methods offer advantages like improved surface finish, enhanced durability, and design flexibility, making them popular in various industries, including automotive and construction.</p></p>
