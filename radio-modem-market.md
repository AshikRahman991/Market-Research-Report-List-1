<p><p>The radio modem market includes various types categorized by frequency bands and licensing requirements. License-free frequency modems operate without regulatory barriers, ideal for personal and small-scale applications. UHF (Ultra High Frequency) modems offer reliable communication over longer distances, suitable for industrial use. Wi-Fi modems leverage existing wireless networks for high-speed data transfer, often used in urban settings. VHF (Very High Frequency) modems excel in marine and aviation communication due to their extended range and clarity, fulfilling diverse operational needs.</p></p>
