<p><p>The miniaturized wireless communication systems market encompasses various transmission types, each serving distinct applications. Radio frequency transmission utilizes electromagnetic waves for effective short-range communication, making it ideal for devices like smartphones and IoT gadgets. Infrared transmission uses light waves for short-range data transfer, commonly seen in remote controls. Microwave transmission operates at higher frequencies, enabling long-distance communication such as satellite links. Lightwave transmission, often used in fiber optics, allows for high-speed data transfer over long distances with minimal signal loss.</p></p>
