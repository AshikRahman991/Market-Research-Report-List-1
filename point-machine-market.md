<p><ul><li>Electric Point Machine</li><li>Electro-hydraulic Point Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Point Machine market includes Electric Point Machines and Electro-hydraulic Point Machines, both crucial for railway switch control. Electric Point Machines utilize electric actuators to position track switches, offering efficiency and lower maintenance. In contrast, Electro-hydraulic Point Machines operate using hydraulic systems, providing greater force and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types serve to enhance safety and operational efficiency in railway systems, with varying preferences based on factors like installation environment and operational demands.</p></p>
