<p><strong>The Semiconductor Test Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Probe Card</li><li>Load Board</li><li>Burn-inBoard(BIB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor test board market comprises three main types: probe cards, load boards, and burn-in boards (BIBs). Probe cards are used in wafer testing to access multiple chips simultaneously, ensuring efficient testing during manufacturing. Load boards facilitate the connection of semiconductor devices to test equipment, enabling functional verification. Burn-in boards are designed for reliability testing, exposing chips to elevated temperatures and voltages to identify early failures. Each type plays a crucial role in semiconductor quality assurance and performance validation.</p></p>
