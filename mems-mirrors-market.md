<p><strong>The MEMS Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1D</li><li>2D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS mirrors market is categorized into 1D and 2D types, depending on the movement capabilities of the mirrors. 1D MEMS mirrors can tilt in a single axis, making them suitable for applications like beam steering in laser projection or optical switching. In contrast, 2D MEMS mirrors can tilt in two orthogonal axes, allowing for more complex applications such as 3D imaging and advanced scanning systems. Both types play critical roles in telecommunications, automotive displays, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
