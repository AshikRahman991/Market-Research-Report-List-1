<p><p>Zirconia in dentistry is classified into three market types based on transparency levels: low, medium, and high. Low transparency zirconia is typically used for frameworks and posterior teeth, offering strength and durability. Medium transparency zirconia balances aesthetics and mechanical properties, making it suitable for anterior crowns and bridges. High transparency zirconia mimics the natural tooth appearance, ideal for restorations in visible areas while remaining strong. Each type serves specific clinical needs, catering to varying aesthetic demands in dental applications.</p></p>
