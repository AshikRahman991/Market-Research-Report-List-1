<p><p>The Transformer Monitoring System market encompasses various technologies to enhance transformer reliability and performance. Bushing monitoring focuses on assessing the condition of insulating bushing components to prevent failures. Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA) devices analyze the gases in transformer oil to identify potential issues, such as insulation breakdown. Partial Discharge (PD) monitoring detects electrical discharges within insulation, indicating deterioration. Other market types may include thermal imaging and vibration analysis, providing comprehensive insights into transformer health and operational efficiency.</p></p>
