<p><p>The sonohysterography catheter market is divided into two primary types: single lumen and double lumen catheters. Single lumen sonohysterography catheters feature one channel for the infusion of saline or contrast media, simplifying the procedure. In contrast, double lumen catheters have two channels, allowing for simultaneous infusion and drainage, which can enhance imaging quality and patient comfort. Both types are utilized in gynecological diagnostics to assess uterine abnormalities and improve clinical outcomes in women’s health care.</p></p>
