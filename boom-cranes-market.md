<p><p>The boom cranes market comprises two primary types: knuckle boom cranes and telescopic boom cranes. Knuckle boom cranes, characterized by their articulated arms, offer flexibility and maneuverability, making them ideal for tight spaces and precise lifting. In contrast, telescopic boom cranes utilize extending sections to reach significant heights and distances, providing exceptional lift capacity for large-scale projects. Both types are essential in construction, shipping, and industrial applications, catering to diverse operational needs across various sectors.</p></p>
