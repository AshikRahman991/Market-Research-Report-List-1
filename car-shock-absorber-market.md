<p><p>The car shock absorber market can be classified by structure into twin-tube and mono-tube types. Twin-tube shock absorbers feature a dual-cylinder design that enhances ride comfort, while mono-tube absorbers provide improved heat dissipation and handling performance due to their single-cylinder construction. By control method, the market is divided into hydraulic and pneumatic types. Hydraulic shock absorbers use fluid dynamics for damping, while pneumatic absorbers utilize air pressure for adjustable ride characteristics, catering to varying driving conditions and preferences.</p></p>
