<p><p>The military ceramic capacitors market primarily consists of two types: Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCC) and Single-Layer Ceramic Capacitors (SLCC). MLCCs are known for their high capacitance in compact sizes, making them ideal for space-constrained applications in military electronics. They exhibit excellent performance under varying environmental conditions. In contrast, SLCCs, with their single-layer design, offer robust reliability and stability for specialized applications. Both types are crucial for defense systems, providing essential functions such as filtering and energy storage.</p></p>
