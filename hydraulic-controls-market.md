<p><ul><li>Pressure Control</li><li>Flow Control</li><li>Direction Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic controls market is divided into three main types: pressure control, flow control, and direction control. Pressure control devices regulate system pressure to prevent overload and ensure safety. Flow control manages the fluid flow rate, optimizing the performance of hydraulic systems. Direction control directs the flow of hydraulic fluid to different parts of the system, enabling precise movement and operation of machinery. Together, these controls enhance efficiency, safety, and functionality in various hydraulic applications across industries.</p></p>
