<p><p>Feed grade valine is an essential amino acid used primarily in animal nutrition. The market is categorized into three types: L-type, D-type, and DL-type. L-type valine is the naturally occurring form preferred for optimal growth and health in livestock. D-type valine is less common and typically used in specialized applications. DL-type valine is a racemic mixture, combining both L and D forms, and is often used for cost-effectiveness and flexibility in formulations, catering to diverse animal feed needs.</p></p>
