<p><p>Cationic Dyeable Polyester (CDP) yarn is classified mainly into three types: DTY (Drawn Textured Yarn), FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn), and Other yarns. DTY yarn features a textured finish, providing softness and bulk, making it ideal for apparel and home textiles. FDY yarn is smooth and strong, offering excellent dyeing characteristics and dimensional stability, suitable for high-performance fabrics. The "Other" category includes specialty yarns that may cater to niche applications, combining the benefits of cationic dyeing with diverse properties.</p></p>
