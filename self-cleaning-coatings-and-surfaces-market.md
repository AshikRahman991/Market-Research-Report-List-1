<p><p>Self-cleaning coatings and surfaces enhance maintenance and cleanliness by utilizing specific properties. Hydrophobic materials repel water, allowing dirt and contaminants to be easily washed away. In contrast, hydrophilic materials attract water, promoting a rinse-off effect. Novel catalysts for self-cleaning materials utilize chemical reactions to break down organic pollutants under light exposure. Electrostatic self-cleaning materials harness electrostatic forces to detach dirt particles, enhancing surface hygiene. Each type plays a crucial role in various applications, from construction to consumer products.</p></p>
