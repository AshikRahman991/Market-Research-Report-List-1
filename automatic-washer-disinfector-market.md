<p><ul><li>Cabinet (Single Chamber) Machines</li><li>Continuous Process Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Washer Disinfector market is primarily divided into two types: Cabinet (Single Chamber) Machines and Continuous Process Machines. Cabinet machines feature a single chamber where instruments are loaded for washing and disinfection, making them ideal for small to medium healthcare settings. In contrast, Continuous Process Machines utilize a continuous loading and unloading system, allowing for high throughput and efficiency, which is suitable for larger facilities that handle a substantial volume of medical instruments. Both types ensure thorough sterilization and compliance with hygiene standards.</p></p>
