<p><p>The steel wire tire cord market is segmented into four main types based on tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) cords are used for standard applications, offering basic durability. High Tensile (HT) cords provide enhanced strength for performance tires, improving resistance to wear and heat. Super Tensile (ST) cords offer even greater strength, suitable for high-performance and heavy-duty tires, while Ultra Tensile (UT) cords deliver exceptional strength and resilience, ideal for specialized applications in extreme conditions. Each type caters to specific performance requirements in tire manufacturing.</p></p>
