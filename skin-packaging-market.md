<p><p>Skin packaging is a popular packaging method used to enhance product visibility and freshness. It is categorized into two main types: carded skin packaging and non-carded skin packaging. Carded skin packaging involves sealing products on a rigid backing card, providing stability and additional branding space. In contrast, non-carded skin packaging uses thin plastic film to tightly seal the product without a backing, allowing for a more compact design. Both types are widely used in food, retail, and consumer goods for protection and presentation.</p></p>
