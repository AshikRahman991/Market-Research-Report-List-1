<p><p>The solar shingle market primarily features two types: silicon photovoltaic (PV) and CIGS (Copper Indium Gallium Selenide) thin-film photovoltaic. Silicon PV shingles are known for their high efficiency and durability, making them a popular choice for residential applications. In contrast, CIGS thin-film technology offers flexibility and lighter weight, allowing for easier integration into various roof designs. While silicon PV shingles dominate in performance, CIGS appeals to niche markets prioritizing aesthetics and versatility, contributing to diverse solar energy solutions.</p></p>
