<p><strong>The Optical Plastic Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Lens</li><li>Aspherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical plastic lens market is primarily categorized into two types: spherical and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses have a uniform curvature, providing consistent magnification but can introduce distortion, especially at the edges. In contrast, aspherical lenses feature a more complex surface profile, reducing optical aberrations and offering a wider field of view. This makes aspherical lenses increasingly popular in applications requiring precision, such as in cameras, eyeglasses, and high-end optics, driving their growth in the market.</p></p>
