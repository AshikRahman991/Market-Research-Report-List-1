<p><ul><li>Film-Based ITO Sensor</li><li>Glass-Based ITO Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ITO (Indium Tin Oxide) sensor market is primarily divided into two types: film-based ITO sensors and glass-based ITO sensors. Film-based ITO sensors are characterized by thin films applied to flexible substrates, offering versatility in applications like touchscreens and displays. In contrast, glass-based ITO sensors utilize a rigid glass substrate, providing durability and high optical clarity, making them ideal for applications like automotive displays and high-end electronics. Both types leverage their conductive and transparent properties for advanced sensing technologies.</p></p>
