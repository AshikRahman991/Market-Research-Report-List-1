<p><p>The windshield lifters market consists of three main types: pneumatically powered, electrically-operated, and mechanical windshield lifters. Pneumatically powered lifters utilize compressed air for smooth operation, making them efficient for high-volume applications. Electrically-operated lifters rely on electric motors for precise control and ease of use, ideal for automated systems. Mechanical lifters, often manually operated, are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for various vehicles and conditions. Each type serves different needs based on functionality, scalability, and user experience.</p></p>
