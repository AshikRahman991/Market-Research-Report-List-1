<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Piston Devices</li><li>Load Distributing Band Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mechanical Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) Devices market includes two main types: Pneumatic Piston Devices and Load Distributing Band Devices. Pneumatic Piston Devices use compressed air to generate rhythmic chest compressions, mimicking manual CPR while ensuring consistent depth and rate. Load Distributing Band Devices, on the other hand, use a flexible band to provide compressions by evenly distributing force across the chest, allowing for effective resuscitation with minimal interruptions, enhancing patient outcomes during cardiac emergencies.</p></p>
