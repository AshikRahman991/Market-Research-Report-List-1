<p><p>The Bridge Wire Rope market is categorized into different types based on the lay direction of the wire strands. Left Regular Lay features strands twisted to the left in a regular pattern, while Left Lang Lay has strands laid more loosely in the same direction. Right Regular Lay involves strands twisted to the right with regular tension, and Right Lang Lay offers a looser twist in that direction. Alternate Lay combines both lay patterns in a single rope, enhancing versatility for various applications.</p></p>
