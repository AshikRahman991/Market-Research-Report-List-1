<p><p>The dispersants market is categorized into three main types: anionic, cationic, and others. Anionic dispersants carry a negative charge, making them effective in stabilizing negatively charged particles in various applications, such as paints and coatings. Cationic dispersants, with a positive charge, are ideal for processes that require stability of positively charged particles, frequently utilized in textiles and paper industries. The "others" category encompasses nonionic and zwitterionic dispersants, which provide versatility across diverse formulations, enhancing dispersion and stability in various systems.</p></p>
