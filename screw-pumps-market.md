<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Screw Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Pump</li><li>Twin-Screw Pump</li><li>Multiple Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw pumps market is categorized into three types: single screw, twin-screw, and multiple screw pumps. Single screw pumps use a single helical screw to transport fluids, making them suitable for viscous liquids. Twin-screw pumps feature two parallel screws that offer higher efficiency and are ideal for a wide range of applications, including oil and chemicals. Multiple screw pumps utilize three or more screws, providing superior reliability and handling of high-pressure systems, commonly used in industrial processes. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various sectors.</p></p>
