<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Active Noise Cancellation Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Reduction</li><li>Back Fed Active Noise Reduction</li><li>Hybrid Active Noise Reduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) technology market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Feedforward Active Noise Reduction** utilizes external microphones to detect ambient noise before it reaches the listener, generating anti-noise signals to cancel it out.</p><p>2. **Feedback Active Noise Reduction** relies on internal microphones positioned within the headphones to analyze sound waves after they enter, allowing real-time adjustments for effective noise cancellation.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Active Noise Reduction** combines both feedforward and feedback systems, enhancing noise cancellation performance by leveraging the strengths of both approaches for better results in various environments.</p></p>
