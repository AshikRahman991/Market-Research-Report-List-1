<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle 3D LiDAR Sensor market comprises two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid-State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses rotating components to emit laser beams and capture 3D spatial data, offering high precision and long-range capabilities but with higher costs and bulkier designs. Solid-State LiDAR, on the other hand, relies on fixed components without moving parts, providing compact, durable, and cost-effective solutions, though often with shorter range and lower resolution. Both are pivotal for autonomous vehicles, enhancing perception and navigation systems.</p></p>
