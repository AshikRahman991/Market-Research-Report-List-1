<p><p>The turntable market features three main types: direct-drive, belt-drive, and idler-wheel turntables. Direct-drive models offer speed stability and quick start-up, making them ideal for DJs. Belt-drive turntables utilize a rubber belt to reduce vibrations, appealing to audiophiles seeking superior sound quality. Idler-wheel turntables operate with a mechanical connection through a wheel, delivering a vintage appeal and unique sound characteristics. Each type caters to different user preferences, from casual listeners to professional artists.</p></p>
