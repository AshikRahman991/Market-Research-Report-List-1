<p><ul><li>Natural Ventilation System</li><li>Forced Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Crank Case Ventilation (CCV) System includes two main types: Natural Ventilation and Forced Ventilation. </p><p>A Natural Ventilation System relies on pressure differentials created during engine operation to allow gases to escape, using simple passages without additional energy. In contrast, a Forced Ventilation System employs mechanical components like blowers or fans to actively expel crankcase gases, enhancing efficiency and performance. Both systems aim to reduce emissions and maintain optimal engine pressure while ensuring effective ventilation.</p></p>
