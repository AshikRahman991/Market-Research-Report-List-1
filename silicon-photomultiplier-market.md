<p><p>The Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM) market is categorized into two main types: Monocoque Type and Array Type. The Monocoque Type features a single, compact design that enhances sensitivity and reduces noise, making it suitable for applications requiring high precision. In contrast, the Array Type consists of multiple interconnected SiPMs, providing higher photon detection efficiency and better scalability. Both types are utilized in medical imaging, particle physics, and astrophysics, catering to diverse detection requirements and performance specifications.</p></p>
