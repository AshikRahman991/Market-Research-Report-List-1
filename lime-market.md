<p><p>The lime market comprises two main types: quick lime and hydrated lime. Quick lime, produced by heating limestone, is primarily used in construction, metal processing, and as a chemical feedstock. Hydrated lime, created by adding water to quick lime, is utilized in water treatment, soil stabilization, and environmental applications. Both types play critical roles in industries like agriculture, waste management, and manufacturing, driven by their properties that aid in neutralization, purification, and stabilization processes.</p></p>
