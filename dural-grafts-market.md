<p><p>The dural grafts market consists of four key types: </p><p>1. **Xenogeneic Dural Graft** originates from a different species, providing a suitable alternative for brain surgeries. </p><p>2. **Synthetic Dural Graft** is engineered from materials like polymers, ensuring consistency and minimizing infection risk. </p><p>3. **Autologous Dural Graft** is harvested from the patient's own tissue, reducing rejection chances and enhancing compatibility. </p><p>4. **Allogeneic Dural Graft** is derived from human donors, allowing for easier availability while still being biocompatible for patients.</p></p>
