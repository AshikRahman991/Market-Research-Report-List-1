<p><strong>The LED Driving Power Supply Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Power Supply</li><li>Built in Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED driving power supply market is categorized into two primary types: external power supplies and built-in power supplies. External power supplies are standalone units that connect to LEDs, offering flexibility in design and installation. They typically provide a regulated output voltage or current and are easy to replace or upgrade. In contrast, built-in power supplies are integrated directly within the LED fixture, saving space and simplifying wiring. This option often leads to enhanced energy efficiency and a more streamlined appearance for lighting solutions.</p></p>
