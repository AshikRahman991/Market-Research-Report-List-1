<p><ul><li>SC GaAs</li><li>SI GaAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) wafer market is primarily segmented into two types: Semi-Insulating (SC) GaAs and Semi- Conducting (SI) GaAs. SC GaAs wafers, characterized by their high resistivity, are commonly used in optoelectronic applications and microwave technology due to their excellent thermal stability and minimal background noise. In contrast, SI GaAs wafers possess lower resistivity, making them suitable for electronic devices like transistors and integrated circuits, where effective charge transport is crucial for performance and efficiency.</p></p>
