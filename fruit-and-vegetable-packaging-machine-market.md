<p><p>The fruit and vegetable packaging machine market can be categorized into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical packaging machines utilize a space-efficient design, allowing for high-speed packaging of products standing upright, often suitable for bags or pouches. They excel in minimizing floor space and optimizing productivity. In contrast, horizontal packaging machines enable side-loading of products, ideal for larger items or bulk packaging. They provide versatility in packaging formats and are commonly used for pre-packaged items, enhancing visibility on retail shelves.</p></p>
