<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) splitter market is divided into passive and active types. Passive splitters provide a straightforward approach by simply separating power and data without any intelligence, making them cost-effective and easy to use for devices with fixed power needs. In contrast, active splitters intelligently manage power distribution, allowing for dynamic adaptation to varying device requirements. This results in better efficiency and protection against power surges, catering to devices with more complex power needs in network setups.</p></p>
