<p><ul><li>Linear Magnetic Encoders</li><li>Rotary Magnetic Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical encoder market is primarily divided into two types: linear magnetic encoders and rotary magnetic encoders. Linear magnetic encoders translate linear motion into digital signals, essential for applications requiring precise position tracking, such as CNC machines and robotics. Rotary magnetic encoders, on the other hand, measure the rotational position and speed of an object, making them crucial for automation and control systems in industries like manufacturing and automotive. Both types enhance accuracy and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
