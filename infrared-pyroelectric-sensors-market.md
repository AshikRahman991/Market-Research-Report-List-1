<p><p>The infrared pyroelectric sensors market comprises two main types: single-channel and multi-channel detectors. Single-channel detectors focus on detecting infrared radiation from a single source, making them ideal for applications like motion detection or temperature sensing in limited environments. In contrast, multi-channel detectors can sense multiple sources simultaneously, allowing for more complex applications such as environmental monitoring and advanced security systems. These detectors enhance sensitivity, accuracy, and response time, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
