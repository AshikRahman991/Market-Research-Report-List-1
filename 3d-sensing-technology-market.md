<p><p>The 3D sensing technology market encompasses various methods, including Stereoscopic Vision, which uses dual cameras to perceive depth by mimicking human sight. Structured Light Pattern projects a light pattern onto an object to capture depth information based on deformation. Time of Flight measures the time it takes for a laser pulse to return after bouncing off an object, offering precise distance calculations. Ultrasound employs sound waves to gather spatial data. Other technologies include laser scanning and radar, enhancing diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
