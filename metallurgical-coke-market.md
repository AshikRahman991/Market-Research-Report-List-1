<p><p>Metallurgical coke is classified into several types based on size and application in steelmaking. Blast furnace coke is used in iron production and needs high strength and low reactivity. Nut coke, a larger size, is suitable for converters. Buckwheat coke is smaller and serves as a filler material. Coke breeze consists of fine particles, often utilized as a fuel or in sintering processes. Coke dust, the smallest form, may be used for various energy applications or as a reducing agent in metallurgical processes.</p></p>
