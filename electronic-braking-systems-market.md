<p><ul><li>Disc EBS</li><li>Drum EBS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Braking System (EBS) market includes Disc EBS and Drum EBS types. Disc EBS uses brake discs and calipers, offering superior heat dissipation, consistent performance, and shorter stopping distances, making it ideal for high-performance and heavy-duty vehicles. Drum EBS employs brake drums and shoes, providing cost-effectiveness and durability, suitable for lighter vehicles or applications with lower braking demands. Both systems enhance safety, control, and efficiency through electronic integration, but Disc EBS is preferred for advanced performance, while Drum EBS remains a practical, economical choice.</p></p>
