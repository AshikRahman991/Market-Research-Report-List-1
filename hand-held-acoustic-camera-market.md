<p><strong>The Hand Held Acoustic Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Microphones</li><li>Traditional Array Microphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld acoustic camera market is primarily segmented into two types: MEMS microphones and traditional array microphones. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphones are compact, offering high sensitivity and low power consumption, making them ideal for portable applications. In contrast, traditional array microphones consist of multiple elements arranged to capture sound from different directions, providing enhanced spatial resolution and fidelity. Both types cater to various applications, including industrial testing, environmental monitoring, and wildlife studies, each serving specific user needs.</p></p>
