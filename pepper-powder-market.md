<p><ul><li>White Pepper Powder</li><li>Black Pepper Powder</li><li>Green Prickley Ash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pepper powder market includes three primary types: white pepper powder, black pepper powder, and green prickly ash. White pepper powder is made from ripe pepper berries with the outer skin removed, offering a milder flavor. Black pepper powder comes from unripe berries, providing a robust and pungent taste, commonly used in various cuisines. Green prickly ash, often confused with traditional pepper, offers a unique citrusy aroma and numbing sensation, catering to gourmet dishes and specialty culinary applications.</p></p>
