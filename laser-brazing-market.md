<p><p>Laser brazing is a precise joining technique used in various applications, primarily categorized into fillet welding and lap welding. Fillet welding involves joining two metals at an angle, creating a triangular cross-section, ideal for corner and joint applications. Lap welding, on the other hand, utilizes overlapping pieces, allowing for a strong bond over a larger surface area. Both methods benefit from laser technology's ability to deliver concentrated heat, enhancing efficiency and reducing distortion in the welded materials.</p></p>
