<p><ul><li>Single-Channel Pipettes</li><li>Multichannel Pipettes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adjustable pipettes market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed for precise liquid handling in individual wells or samples, making them ideal for tasks that require high accuracy and flexibility. In contrast, multichannel pipettes allow simultaneous liquid dispensing across multiple wells, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput applications, such as ELISA or PCR setups. Both types cater to the needs of laboratories, facilitating various research and diagnostic procedures.</p></p>
