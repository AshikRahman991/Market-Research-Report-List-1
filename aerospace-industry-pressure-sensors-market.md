<p><p>The aerospace industry employs various types of pressure sensors crucial for monitoring and controlling aircraft systems. Absolute pressure sensors measure the total pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, ensuring accurate readings in altitude systems. Differential pressure sensors gauge the pressure difference between two points, vital for aerodynamic assessments. Relative pressure sensors measure pressure concerning atmospheric pressure, useful in environmental monitoring. Other types may include specialized sensors tailored for unique applications, enhancing performance and safety in aerospace operations.</p></p>
