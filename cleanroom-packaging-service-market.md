<p><p>The Cleanroom Packaging Service Market comprises two primary types: Dust-free Sterilization Packaging and Dust-free Packaging. Dust-free Sterilization Packaging is designed to maintain a sterile environment for sensitive products, utilizing methods like gamma radiation or ethylene oxide to ensure microbiological safety. In contrast, Dust-free Packaging focuses on creating an uncontaminated space to protect products from particulates and contaminants, crucial for industries such as electronics and pharmaceuticals. Both types prioritize contamination control, ensuring product integrity and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
