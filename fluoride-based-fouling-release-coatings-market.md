<p><p>Fluoride-based fouling release coatings are specialized surface treatments designed to prevent the accumulation of marine organisms on vessels and structures. In the market, two primary types exist: those containing biocides and biocide-free options. The biocide-based coatings incorporate antimicrobial agents to actively inhibit biofouling, offering enhanced protection but potentially raising environmental concerns. Conversely, biocide-free coatings rely on slick surfaces and low-friction properties to deter organism attachment, representing a more eco-friendly approach while still providing effective fouling release.</p></p>
