<p><p>The ATV air filter market primarily includes foam-based and paper-based filters. Foam-based filters are known for their durability, reusability, and ability to trap fine particles, making them ideal for off-road and dusty environments. They are washable and require regular maintenance. Paper-based filters, on the other hand, are disposable, cost-effective, and provide efficient filtration for standard riding conditions. They are lightweight and easy to replace but less suitable for extreme environments. Both types cater to different ATV usage needs, balancing performance, maintenance, and cost.</p></p>
