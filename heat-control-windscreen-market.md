<p><ul><li>Heated Wire Windshield</li><li>Heated Coated Windshield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat control windscreen market primarily consists of two types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields feature fine wires embedded within the glass, providing direct heat to melt ice and snow. In contrast, heated coated windshields utilize a transparent conductive layer applied to the glass surface, efficiently distributing heat across the pane. Both types enhance visibility and safety in cold climates, catering to the growing demand for advanced automotive technologies in vehicles.</p></p>
