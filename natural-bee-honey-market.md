<p><p>The natural bee honey market primarily comprises two types: polyfloral honey and monofloral honey. Polyfloral honey is produced from the nectar of various flowers, resulting in a diverse flavor profile and potential health benefits, appealing to consumers seeking variety. In contrast, monofloral honey comes from a single type of flower, offering distinct tastes and attributes tied to that specific nectar source. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and are vital segments in the growing health and wellness market.</p></p>
