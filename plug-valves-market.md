<p><p>Plug valves are quarter-turn valves that utilize a cylindrical or conically tapered plug to control fluid flow. Designed for on/off service, these valves provide a robust solution in various applications, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing. Their simple design allows for easy maintenance and low-pressure drops, contributing to their increasing adoption across industries.</p><p>The Plug Valves Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period. <p><p>The plug valve market includes several types, each designed for specific applications. Non-lubricated plug valves rely on tight tolerances for sealing, making them ideal for clean services. Lubricated plug valves have a lubrication mechanism that enhances their sealing and reduces wear, suitable for more demanding conditions. Eccentric plug valves feature an offset design for smoother flow and less friction, while expanding plug valves adjust their size for better sealing under pressure. Each type caters to different industrial needs.</p></p>
