<p><p>The intrauterine surgery adhesion barrier market includes three main types: film formulations, gel formulations, and liquid formulations. Film formulations are solid, thin layers applied to prevent adhesion, offering controlled release and ease of application. Gel formulations provide a viscous barrier that conforms to tissue surfaces, enhancing adhesion prevention while maintaining moisture. Liquid formulations allow for easy distribution within the surgical site, effectively coating tissues and reducing the risk of adhesions. Each type serves to improve patient outcomes by minimizing post-surgical complications.</p></p>
