<p><p>The infrared night-vision scope market is primarily divided into two types: single tube and double tube scopes. Single tube infrared night-vision scopes utilize a single optical path, making them more compact and lightweight, ideal for portable usage. In contrast, double tube scopes feature separate channels for each eye, providing enhanced depth perception and a more natural viewing experience. Both types are widely used in various applications, including military, law enforcement, and recreational activities, catering to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
