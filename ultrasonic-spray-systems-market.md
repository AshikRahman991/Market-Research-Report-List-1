<p><p>The Ultrasonic Spray Systems market is primarily divided into Rotary-Type and Flat-Type systems. Rotary-Type Ultrasonic Spray Systems utilize a rotating mechanism to atomize liquid coatings, providing uniform coverage and fine droplets, making them suitable for intricate applications. In contrast, Flat-Type Ultrasonic Spray Systems employ a stationary design, delivering a consistent spray pattern across wider surfaces, ideal for large-area coatings. Both systems leverage ultrasonic technology to enhance material application efficiency and improve surface adhesion.</p></p>
