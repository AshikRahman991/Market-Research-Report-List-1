<p><p>The Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) market can be categorized into two main types: CMOOC and XMOOC. CMOOCs, or Connectivist MOOCs, focus on collaborative learning through peer interactions and emphasize co-creation of knowledge. They promote networking and community engagement. In contrast, XMOOCs are more traditional, resembling structured classroom experiences with a focus on content delivery through lectures and assessments. They are often associated with specific institutions and emphasize formal learning outcomes. Both enhance accessibility to education globally.</p></p>
