<p><p>The medical moist wound dressings market comprises various types designed to promote healing by maintaining a moist environment. Foam dressings provide cushioning and absorb drainage, while alginate dressings, made from seaweed, offer high absorbency and support the formation of a gel upon contact with exudate. Film dressings create a protective barrier while allowing moisture vapor transmission. Hydrocolloid dressings swell in the presence of exudate, forming a gel that supports healing. Hydrogel dressings provide moisture and cooling, ideal for dry wounds.</p></p>
