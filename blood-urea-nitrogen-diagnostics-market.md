<p><p>The Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN) diagnostics market includes various testing methods like ELISA, colorimetric, and spectrophotometric assays. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) offers high specificity and sensitivity for measuring BUN levels via antibodies. Colorimetric assays rely on chemical reactions that produce color changes proportional to urea concentration, providing quick results. Spectrophotometric methods measure the intensity of light absorbed by the sample at specific wavelengths, allowing precise quantification of urea in blood samples, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
