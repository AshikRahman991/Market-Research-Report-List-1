<p><strong>The Replacement Rearview Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Camera</li><li>CMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The replacement rearview mirrors market comprises two primary types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and sensitivity in low-light conditions, making them suitable for enhanced visibility. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer advantages such as lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integrated image processing capabilities, which are beneficial for real-time applications. Both types enhance driver safety and provide clearer rear visibility, driving market growth.</p></p>
