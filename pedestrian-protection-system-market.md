<p><p>The Pedestrian Protection System market is categorized into two main types: Passive and Active systems. Passive Pedestrian Protection Systems are designed to minimize injury by using safety features, such as crumple zones, energy-absorbing materials, and design elements that reduce impact severity during a collision. In contrast, Active Pedestrian Protection Systems employ technology to prevent accidents before they occur, utilizing sensors, cameras, and automatic braking systems to detect pedestrians and mitigate potential collisions, enhancing overall road safety.</p></p>
