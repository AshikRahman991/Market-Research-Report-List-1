<p><ul><li>Single Shake Bed</li><li>Double Shake Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual hospital beds market comprises primarily two types: single shake beds and double shake beds. Single shake beds offer basic adjustments, allowing caregivers to elevate the head or foot of the bed for patient comfort and care. In contrast, double shake beds provide more advanced positioning options, enabling adjustments at both the head and foot sections simultaneously. This dual functionality enhances patient comfort and accessibility, making it easier for healthcare providers to perform medical procedures and routines.</p></p>
