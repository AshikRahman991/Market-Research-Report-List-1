<p><p>Silicon wafer auto clean equipment encompasses two primary market types: wet clean and dry cleaning. Wet clean processes utilize liquid chemicals and rinsing solutions to remove contaminants from wafers, ensuring a high level of cleanliness essential for semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, dry cleaning employs gas-phase chemicals or plasma to clean wafers without the use of liquids, which can reduce the risk of contamination from residues. Both methods are critical for maintaining the quality and performance of silicon wafers in electronic applications.</p></p>
