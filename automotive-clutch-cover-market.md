<p><ul><li>Coil Spring Clutch Cover</li><li>Diaphragm Spring Clutch Cover</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive clutch cover market includes various types such as coil spring and diaphragm spring clutch covers. Coil spring clutch covers utilize multiple coil springs to provide engagement and disengagement, offering a classic design suitable for various vehicle types. In contrast, diaphragm spring clutch covers use a single diaphragm spring, providing a more compact and lightweight solution. Diaphragm clutches typically offer improved performance and smoother operation, making them popular in modern vehicles. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance needs and design preferences.</p></p>
