<p><p>Film permeability testers are essential tools used to assess the barrier properties of packaging films against various gases. The market is categorized mainly into three types: water vapor, oxygen, and other gases. Water vapor permeability testers measure how much moisture can pass through a material, which is crucial for food packaging. Oxygen permeability testers evaluate the film's ability to prevent oxygen ingress, essential for preserving freshness. Other gas testers cater to specific needs, assessing the permeability of gases like carbon dioxide and nitrogen.</p></p>
