<p><p>The PCR PET resin market is categorized into two primary types: modified PCR (m-PCR) and conventional PCR (c-PCR). m-PCR is produced by blending recycled PET with virgin materials, enhancing properties for specialized applications while promoting sustainability. In contrast, c-PCR consists solely of recycled PET, often used in standard applications where cost and recyclability are prioritized. Both types contribute to reducing environmental impact, supporting the circular economy, and meeting increasing demand for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
