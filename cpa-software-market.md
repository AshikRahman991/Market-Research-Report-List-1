<p><p>CPA software markets can be categorized into three types: On-Premise, Web-Based, and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software is installed locally on users' machines, offering higher control but requiring maintenance and updates. Web-Based solutions are accessible through browsers, providing flexibility and ease of access without the need for installation, though they may depend on internet connectivity. Cloud-Based software operates remotely on servers, allowing for scalable access from multiple devices, promoting collaboration and automatic updates while minimizing local resource usage.</p></p>
