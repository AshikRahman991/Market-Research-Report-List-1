<p><ul><li>Hub Motor</li><li>Mid Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bicycle drive motor market primarily features two main types: hub motors and mid motors. Hub motors are integrated into either the front or rear wheel, providing direct power for easy installation and a simple design. They are commonly used in commuter bikes for their reliability. Mid motors, located near the bike's pedals, offer better weight distribution and improved handling, delivering efficient power to the bike’s drivetrain. This type is favored for performance-oriented e-bikes due to its enhanced control and responsiveness.</p></p>
