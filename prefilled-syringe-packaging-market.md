<p><ul><li>Glass Material</li><li>Plastic Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prefilled syringe packaging market is segmented into two main types based on material: glass and plastic. Glass prefilled syringes are favored for their inert properties, providing excellent barrier protection and ensuring drug stability. They are commonly used for sensitive biologics or high-precision medications. Conversely, plastic prefilled syringes, often made from polymer materials, offer lightweight, shatterproof alternatives with enhanced design flexibility. While they are cost-effective and suitable for various injectable drugs, they may have limitations in terms of compatibility with certain medications.</p></p>
