<p><strong>The 90 Degree Elbow Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>90degree Short Radius Elbow</li><li>90 degree Long Radius Elbow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 90 Degree Elbow market is divided into two main types: short radius elbows and long radius elbows. Short radius elbows have a curvature that is equal to the diameter of the pipe, making them ideal for tight spaces and quick direction changes. In contrast, long radius elbows feature a curvature that is one-and-a-half times the pipe diameter, allowing for smoother fluid flow, reducing pressure drop. Both types are essential in various piping systems across industries, ensuring efficient liquid and gas transport.</p></p>
